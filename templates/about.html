Frequently Asked Questions

Are you getting paid to do this?

Nope! We’re entirely volunteer run. Proceeds go directly to Planned Parenthood; we just collect the good vibes knowing you’re helping us fight for reproductive rights.

I don’t have a period. Can I still #SyncWithUs and support Planned Parenthood?

Absolutely! You can still pledge to support your sweetie, spouse, best friend, family member, colleague, or anyone else you care about. Anybody can #SyncWithUs.

Are you affiliated with Planned Parenthood?

No, but it’s still legit. Our team has worked closely with Planned Parenthood to make this happen, partnering with Crowdrise, an official donation platform for Planned Parenthood, to host our fundraiser.

What is Planned Parenthood doing with this money?

Your pledge will go directly to Planned Parenthood Health Services. Your donation helps to keep the doors open for the 661 Planned Parenthood health centers around the country that provide critical health screenings, STI treatments and reproductive services. Learn more about where your money is going by heading over to Planned Parenthood Health Services.

How do I cancel my recurring donation? (and/or other donation-specific questions)

Our trusted donation platform partner, Crowdrise, has those questions covered for you. Feel free to reach out to us at hello@syncwithus.org if you have any questions not addressed on their website.
